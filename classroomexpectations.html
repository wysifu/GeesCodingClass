Classroom Expectations

  1. respect yourself and others

    2. - Treat people the way you would like to be treated

    - Show respect for yourself and others at all times

    - Be a kind person


  2. imagination is key, but engagement is crucial

    3. - Imagination is key in programming because we can tell the computer to do whatever we want, however, engagement in learning the language and creating projects is crucial in learning to program.

    - Actively listen and pay attention to detail.

    - If you have questions, ask and seek the answer.


  3. come prepared

    4. - Everyday, come prepared with pens and pencils, a notebook, headphones, and a flash drive.

    - Be ready to interact with computers and search the Internet for ways to accomplish your goal.


  4. stay ready, so you dont have to get ready

    5. - Take notes while learning so you can always easily refer back to them. There is no cheating in programming.

    - While studying and taking notes, open a live server to learn new concepts and topics in real time

    - Create small projects and store them in a folder on the web to show others. You will not have to relearn anything you use often.


  5. 110 percent

    6. - Always give maximum effort, focus, and concentration towards learning to program.

    - Put learning before just trying to get good grades. These skills will take you places you can't see right now.

    - Aim for quality work. The saying goes, "Quality Over Quantity"... - ancient mayan proverb


  6. be accountable

    7. - Hold yourself accountable for your own actions.

    - Evaluate how you spend your time.

    - Be honest.
